Rhyzo is developing a device that helps people grow mushrooms at home. It controls humidity, lighting, and airflow to ensure maximum yields for whatever species you desire to grow.

Modularity

Through the LCD screen (and in the future, an App) you are able to control humidity, lighting, and airflow, creating schedules and cycles that can be tweaked to accommodate any mushroom species that you want to grow.
