- Rhyzo Full Fruiting System -

A paradigm shift in mushroom cultivation.
Expanded environmental control for fine-tuning and maximizing yields.

This modular solution seamlessly integrates with a DIY fruiting chamber, overseeing critical parameters: humidity, temperature, lighting, and misting. Achieve optimal fruiting with features that set us apart, like direct mycelium misting and sub-ambient cooling, all managed through our simple and privacy-oriented app. Tailor conditions to specific species and capture growth progress through the optional camera module's timelapse capabilities. Experience the synergy of modularity, affordability, and user-friendly functionality, redefining your approach to mushroom cultivation with convenience.

Perfect to transform terrariums, storage totes, and other rigid containers into fruiting chambers. Want a fruiting system as a centerpiece for your Livingroom? Combine this system with our luxury chamber to merge utility with aesthetics.
